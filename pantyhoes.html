Pantyhoes

Pantyhoes is a publication / community that was created by myself and a friend in the summer of 2014. Sick of the lack of confidence in girls, we created this community in hopes to lift women up and express that no one is "normal" nor "perfect.

The Pantyhoes publication is released biannually and is composed of poems, drawings, thoughts, etc. We look to inspire and bring new ideas to the discussion. Aside from our publication release parties, we often host charity events to raise money for causes we believe need attention.

Pantyhoes more than anything is a social experiment - it's always evolving into something new.
